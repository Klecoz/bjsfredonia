About Us

Opened in December 1933, BJ’s is one of the oldest bars this half of New York State. With the best wings, some of the best burgers, BBQ, and the absolute best beer selection in town, we have something to cater to everyone's tastes! There's also an extensive Bourbon collection, one of the only ones in town. Cheap prices and no bullshit, that’s what BJ’s promises.
